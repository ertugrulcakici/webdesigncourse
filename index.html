Location: Prague
At first times of our erasmus, my friend and I decided to go on a spontaneous trip to Prague for two days. Despite the short stay, we were able to see and experience so much of this stunning city. We walked through the winding streets of the Old Town, admiring the historic buildings and charming cafes. We visited the famous Charles Bridge and were captivated by the breathtaking views of the Vltava River. In the evening, we dined on traditional Czech cuisine and enjoyed a night out at a local bar. Although our time in Prague was brief, it was a trip that we will always cherish and remember fondly.
Location: Italy(Venice)
During our time in Erasmus, my friend and I decided to take a trip to Venice. It was one of the most enchanting cities we had ever visited. We wandered through the winding streets and narrow canals, marveling at the beauty of the old buildings and bridges. We took a gondola ride and were serenaded by a charming gondolier as we glided through the shimmering waters. We also visited the famous Piazza San Marco, where we were mesmerized by the intricate details of the Basilica di San Marco and the ornate architecture of the Doge's Palace. We indulged in delicious Italian food and gelato, and soaked up the lively atmosphere of the city. It was a magical trip that we will always remember fondly.
Location: Italy(Florence)
Florence, the birthplace of Renaissance, was a city that fascinated me from the moment I stepped into its historic center. Walking through the narrow streets lined with palaces and cathedrals, I felt as though I was transported back in time to the 15th century when Florence was at its peak. My friend and I spent our days admiring the masterpieces of Michelangelo, Leonardo da Vinci, and Botticelli in the Uffizi Gallery and the Accademia Gallery. We also enjoyed strolling through the Boboli Gardens, where we were greeted with stunning views of the city. In the evenings, we savored delicious Tuscan cuisine and watched the sunset over the Arno River from the Ponte Vecchio. Florence was a city that left a lasting impression on me, and I knew I would always cherish the memories of our trip.
Location: Italy(Bologna)
Bologna is a city in northern Italy, known for its medieval architecture and vibrant culture. It's also home to the oldest university in the world, the University of Bologna, which was founded in 1088. During our time in Italy, my friend and I decided to take a day trip to this historic city. We strolled through the narrow streets and admired the beautiful architecture of the old buildings. We also visited the famous Piazza Maggiore, where we were captivated by the stunning Basilica di San Petronio and the Fountain of Neptune. In the evening, we dined on traditional Italian cuisine and enjoyed a night out at a local bar. It was a trip that we will always remember fondly.
Location: Italy(San Gimignano)
San Gimignano is a charming medieval town located in the heart of Tuscany, Italy. With its stunning views of the rolling hills and lush vineyards, it's easy to see why it's called the "Town of Fine Towers." During our trip to Italy, my friends and I decided to take a day trip to this picturesque town. We strolled through the narrow streets and explored the ancient architecture of the town, admiring the medieval towers and the beautiful frescoes. We also sampled some of the famous local wines and indulged in the delicious Tuscan cuisine. As the sun began to set, we sat on a hilltop terrace and watched the breathtaking sunset, feeling grateful for the opportunity to experience such a magical place.
